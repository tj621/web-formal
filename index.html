团队介绍


同济大学徐立鸿教授研究团队，为国家级重点学科“控制理论与控制工程”一级学科、国家设施农业工程中心， 教育部设施农业网上合作研究中心下设置的研究室，隶属于同济大学电子与信息工程学院，主要从事“温室环 境建模与控制”，“温室作物数字化与可视化”等领域研究工作。

研究团队近5年来，负责或参与完成了多项国家自然科学基金项目、国家科技支撑项目和863项目，取得了一大批 科研成果，部分成果达到国际国内领先水平，研究成果曾获国家级科技进步二等奖、教育部科技进步一等奖。近 5年发表相关高端论文33篇，其中SCI检索论文7篇，EI检索论文近28篇。

Tongji university professor li-hong xu team for the national key disciplines "control theory and control engineering" level of discipline, national agricultural facilities, engineering center, the ministry of education facilities agriculture cooperative research center set up under the online research, affiliated to the electronics and information engineering college of tongji university, mainly engaged in "greenhouse environment modeling and control", "greenhouse crop digitalization and visualization in the fields of research.

In recent 5 years, our team is responsible for or participated in completed a number of national natural science fund project, national science and technology support project and project 863, achieved a large number of scientific research achievements, some achievements reached the international and domestic leading level, research was awarded the national scientific and technological progress second prize, the ministry of education science and technology progress award. high major papers published in recent five years, including 7 SCI retrieval theses, EI retrieval nearly 28 papers.
